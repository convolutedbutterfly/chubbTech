Your ideas, online

I know that building a website can feel overwhelming — but it doesn’t have to be. I work closely with you to understand what you actually need, then create a tailored solution that feels just right for your business. No unnecessary features, no jargon — just a straightforward, collaborative process that puts you in control and brings your ideas to life online.

Discovery

We start by getting clear on what you need, what’s working (and what’s not), and where you want to go. This is where I ask questions, take notes, and really get to know your business.

Design

Once we’ve mapped things out, I’ll begin designing a look and structure that fits your brand, your goals, and your audience. You’ll see mockups, give feedback, and help shape the direction

Develop

With the design locked in, I’ll build your site and make sure everything works just as it should — on mobile, desktop, and everywhere in between.

Deploy

We’ll walk through the final version together, make any last tweaks, and I’ll get everything ready to go live. I’ll also make sure you’re comfortable managing your site, or support you with ongoing updates if you’d prefer.
